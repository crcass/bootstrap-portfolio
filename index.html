About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! Originally from the Midwest, I've called Texas home for the past 12 years. I am a life-long musician (piano & percussion) with a Bachelor of Music degree from the University of Iowa (Go Hawks!) and Master of Music degree from the University of Oklahoma (Boomer Sooner!).


When I'm not working or coding, you can find me at a concert or out in nature. I'm also working to get more stamps in my passport and I'll always say "yes" to an adventure!


My secret skill is making desserts, especially homemade ice creams. I love surprising my friends & family with their favorite flavors and I'm always looking for my next great ice cream experiment!
